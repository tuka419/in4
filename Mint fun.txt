#BBC helps to free suspected migrants stuck in refrigerated lorry in France
Europe migrant crisis
Six suspected migrants  been rescued from the back of a lorry in France, after the BBC helped track them down and alert the police.
The four Vietnamese women and two Iraqi women were trapped inside, panicking and struggling to breathe. One of them spoke to the BBC from inside the lorry.
